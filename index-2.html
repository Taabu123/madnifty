HOW TO READ THIS
CVD (Cumulative Volume Delta) = Total buy-side volume minus sell-side volume. Rising CVD with rising price = strong bullish. Rising CVD with falling price = institutional accumulation (hidden buying).

Institutional Absorption = When OI increases significantly (+20% vs avg) BUT price barely moves (<0.3%) — institutions are absorbing retail orders. This is stealth positioning before a big move.

Long Absorption = Large PE OI build-up + price stable = institutions buying puts as hedge = BEARISH signal.
Short Absorption = Large CE OI build-up + price stable = institutions writing calls = they expect price below that level.
